Required Legal Benefits

As a compliant agency, we ensure all placements adhere to the following mandatory standards for worker welfare.

🍱 Free Food & Accommodation

Employers in Saudi Arabia, Jordan, and Kuwait are legally required to provide clean, safe living quarters and three nutritious meals a day at no cost to the worker.

🏥 Full Medical Insurance

Comprehensive health coverage must be paid entirely by the employer for the full duration of the 2-year contract.

✈️ Free Air Travel

Employers provide a free one-way ticket from Addis Ababa at the start of employment, and a free return ticket upon successful completion of the 2-year contract.

💳 Traceable Monthly Payments

Salaries must be processed electronically: via Musaned/WPS for Saudi Arabia, and monitored via the Public Authority for Manpower (PAM) for Kuwait.

⏱️ Rest Periods

Workers are entitled to a minimum of 1 full day of rest per week, in addition to standard daily rest periods.
